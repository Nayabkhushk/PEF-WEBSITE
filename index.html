Welcome to PEF Mehran Chapter

Pakistan Engineering Forum (PEF) is a platform for aspiring engineers to connect, learn, and grow. We aim to enhance the academic and professional journey of students through industrial visits, workshops, and leadership opportunities.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide expert engineering services, ensuring the highest quality in every project, and to foster continuous development of engineering professionals through comprehensive training and development programs.

Vision

To be the largest engineers community in Pakistan, offering cutting-edge solutions tailored to meet specific needs, and facilitating networking and collaboration among engineers to promote innovation and professional growth.
